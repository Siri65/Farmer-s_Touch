Know About the Soil Compositions

We provide all the characteristics of different vegtables soil properties...

Brinjal

Soil Texture: Sandy loam or loamy soils.

pH Level: pH range of 5.5 to 7.0.

Temperature: 70°F to 95°F (21°C - 35°C).

Nutrient Content: Incorporating compost or well-rotted manure into the soil before planting can provide nutrients and improve soil.

Climate: Grown under low temperature during the cool season. It is a warm season crop and susceptible to severe frost.

Cabbage

Soil Texture: Sandy loam or loamy soils.

pH Level: pH range of 6.0 and 7.5.

Temperature: 45°F - 75°F (7°C to 24°C).

Nutrient Content: Adequate levels of essential nutrients such as nitrogen, phosphorus, and potassium are crucial.

Climate: Grown in large areas having a cool and moist climate.

Tomato

Soil Texture: Sandy loam or loamy soils.

pH Level: pH range of 6.0 and 6.8.

Temperature: 60°F to 85°F (16°C - 29°C)

Nutrient Content: Incorporating compost, aged manure, or balanced organic fertilizers into the soil before planting.

Climate: Grown under warm and humid climate, such as Coastal climate.

Bottle Guard

Soil Texture: Sandy loam or loamy soils.

pH Level: pH range of 6.0 and 7.5.

Temperature: 70°F to 95°F (21°C - 35°C)

Nutrient Content: Excessive salt content in the soil can inhibit plant growth and development.

Climate: Grown under sun, warm & humid climate.

Potato

Soil Texture: Aerated sandy loam or loamy soils.

pH Level: pH range of 5.0 and 6.5.

Temperature: 60°F to 70°F (15°C - 21°C)

Nutrient Content: Require a balanced supply of nutrients i.e., nitrogen, phosphorus, potassium.

Climate: Grown as a summer crop in the hills and as a winter crop in the tropical and subtropical regions

Leafy vegtables

Soil Texture: Aerated sandy loam or loamy soils.

pH Level: pH range of 6.0 and 7.0.

Temperature: 55°F to 70°F (12°C - 21°C)

Nutrient Content: Require a balanced supply of nutrients i.e., calicum, magnesium, potassium.

Climate: Grown under warm,hot and dry climate.
