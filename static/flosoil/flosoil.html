Know About the Soil Compositions

We provide all the characteristics of different flowers soil properties...

Chrysanthemums

Soil Texture: well-draining loamy soil, with sand, silt, clay.

pH Level: pH range of 6.0 to 7.0.

Temperature: 10 to 27°C(60°F-70°F).

Nutrient Content: Require a balanced supply of nutrients, particularly nitrogen, phosphorus, and potassium.

Climate: Grown in tropical & subtropical climate.

Hibiscus

Soil Texture: Sandy loam or loamy soils with dry to moist, moderately fertile.

pH Level: pH range of 6.0 and 7.5.

Temperature: 80 °C to 70 °C

Nutrient Content: Requires nutrients i.e., nitrogen, phosphorus, and potassium with ratio of 10-10-10 .

Climate: Grown under warm, tropical to subtropical climates.

Lily

Soil Texture: Loamy soils, which has mixture of sand, silt, and clay.

pH Level: pH range of 6.0 and 7.0.

Temperature: 18-22°C to 10-15°C

Nutrient Content: Requires nutrients i.e., nitrogen, phosphorus, and potassium with ratio of 10-10-10.

Climate: Grown under temperate climates with distinct seasons.

Rose

Soil Texture: well-draining loamy soil, with sand, silt, clay.

pH Level: pH range of 6.0 and 7.0.

Temperature: 260 °C to 150 °C

Nutrient Content: Requires nutrients i.e., NPK elements with ratio of 5-10-5

Climate: Grown under sun, warm & humid climate.

Jasmine

Soil Texture: Aerated sandy loam or loamy soils with dry to moist, moderately fertile.

pH Level: pH range of 6.0 and 7.0.

Temperature: 60°F to 75°F (15°C-24°C)

Nutrient Content: Require a balanced supply of nutrients i.e., nitrogen, phosphorus, potassium.

Climate: Grown under moderate climate with warm temperatures.

Grape

Soil Texture: Aerated moderately fertile, dry to moist, well-drained soils.

pH Level: pH range of 6.0 and 7.0.

Temperature: 18 to 20°C

Nutrient Content: Require a balanced supply of nutrients i.e., calicum, magnesium, potassium.

Climate: Grown under temperate climates with distinct seasons.
