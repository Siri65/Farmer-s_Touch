Know About the Seedling Precautions

We provide all the required measures to take care of different fruits...

Guava

Environmental conditions: Sensitive to temperature, humidity, and sunlight may lead to wilting or yellowing leaves.

Germination conditions: The seeds needs to be planted in well-draining soil and maintain consistent moisture levels.

Sunlight: Place them in a location where they can receive at least 6-8 hours of sunlight per day.

Avoid overwatering: Overwatering can lead to root rot and other fungal diseases and pathogens.

Others: Cover the pot with plastic wrap to create a greenhouse effect and increase humidity.

Custard Apple

Environmental conditions: Sensitive to temperature, humidity, and sunlight may lead to wilting or yellowing leaves.

Germination conditions: Pre-planting soaking in water overnight. Scarify seeds to break the coat, aiding moisture penetration.

Sunlight: Best in warm temperatures between 24-29°C. Providing a warm, sunny location.

Avoid overwatering: Overwatering can lead to root rot and other fungal diseases and aphids.

Others: Plant the seeds at a depth of around 1 inch (2.5 cm) in the soil.

Sapota

Environmental conditions: Sensitive to temperature, humidity, and sunlight may lead to wilting or yellowing leaves.

Germination conditions:Scarify or soak the seeds in water overnight before planting to soften the seed coat and promote germination.

Sunlight: Best in warm temperatures between 24-29°C. Provide a warm, sunny location.

Avoid overwatering: Overwatering can lead to root rot, fungal diseases and aphids,mites.

Others: Plant the seeds at a depth of around 1 inch to 1.5 inch(2.5 cm) in the soil.

Banana

Environmental conditions: Sensitive to temperature, humidity, and sunlight may lead to wilting or yellowing leaves.

Germination conditions:soak the seeds in water for 24 hours before planting to soften the seed coat and promote germination.

Sunlight: Best in warm temperatures between 24-29°C. Provide a warm, sunny location.

Avoid overwatering: Overwatering can lead to root rot, fungal diseases and aphids,mites,scales.

Others: Plant the seeds at a depth of around 1/2 inch (1.25 cm) in the soil.

Papaya

Environmental conditions: Sensitive to temperature, humidity, and sunlight may lead to wilting or yellowing leaves.

Germination conditions:Requires warm temperatures and consistent moisture, but Old or damaged seeds may have lower germination.

Sunlight: Best in warm temperatures between 24-29°C. Leave for 6-8 hours in sunlight per day.

Avoid overwatering: Overwatering can lead to root rot, fungal diseases and whiteflies,spider.

Others: Plant the seeds at a depth of around 1/2 inch (1.25 cm) in the soil.

Grape

Environmental conditions: Sensitive to temperature, humidity, and sunlight may lead to wilting or yellowing leaves.

Germination conditions:Soak grape seeds in water for 24 hours before planting to soften the seed coat and promote germination.

Sunlight: Best in warm temperatures between 21-27°C. Provide a warm, sunny location.

Avoid overwatering: Overwatering can lead to root rot, fungal diseases and mites, leafhoppers.

Others: Exposing the seeds to cold temperatures (40°F or 4°C) to improve germination rates.
